Many people do not understand what items are recyclable and which items are not. In fact, 18% of people do not understand this. However, it is crucial to understand which materials can be and which can't as it can cost our planet and lives a great deal. With the advent of climate change and pollution, landfills have increased in size and have taken up a large extent of space but also emitting lots of carbon dioxide and methane as landfill gasses. 90-98% of all landfill gases are carbon dioxide and methane. These two gases are also greenhouse gases which break down layers in our ozone, increasing climate change and global warming by letting in harmful UV rays.
